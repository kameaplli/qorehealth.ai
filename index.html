Frequently asked questions

Is QoreHealth available on iOS and Android?

QoreHealth is currently in Android beta with iOS coming soon. Built with Flutter for native performance on both platforms.

How is QoreHealth different from MyFitnessPal?

QoreHealth tracks 55+ micronutrients (not just macros), supports unlimited family members, includes a 6-phase eczema intelligence platform, syncs with 6+ wearable platforms, parses blood test labs, and analyzes grocery spending. No competitor offers this breadth.

Can I track my child's health?

Yes. QoreHealth is family-first. Add unlimited family members and switch between them with a single swipe. Every metric is scoped to the individual.

How does the eczema AI correlation work?

Our 7-dimension engine analyzes food logs, environmental data, product usage, and flare severity using Bayesian probability, lag analysis (6–72 hours), dose-response modeling, and more. Over time, it identifies your personal triggers with increasing confidence.

Is my health data secure?

All data is encrypted in transit (TLS 1.3) and at rest. JWT authentication with bcrypt hashing. Wearable OAuth tokens encrypted with Fernet (AES-256). We never sell health data.

Can I import data from other apps?

Yes. CSV import from MyFitnessPal, Cronometer, and Apple Health XML exports (streaming parser handles files up to 500MB+).

Is QoreHealth a medical device?

No. QoreHealth provides data-driven insights but is not a substitute for medical advice. Always consult your healthcare provider for medical decisions.
